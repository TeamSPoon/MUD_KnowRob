Web <a href="http://www.w3.org/DesignIssues/diagrams/sweb-stack/2006a.png">layer cake</a> i.e. Linked Data, Unifying Logic and Proof.

of EULER i.e. EYE = Unifying Logic + Explainable Reasoning.
